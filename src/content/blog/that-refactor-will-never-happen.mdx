---
title: That Refactor Will Never Happen
description: For the vast majority of stuff that you work on, there won't be any major refactoring. It will continue in its current form until the company dies or you leave.
publishDate: 2023-10-23
---

Perhaps this is some excessive navel-gazing in the shadow of my [former employer Convoy going belly-up](https://www.geekwire.com/2023/convoy-collapse-read-ceos-memo-detailing-sudden-shutdown-of-seattle-trucking-startup/), but I've been thinking this for a while.

**Bottom line**: The code you're complaining about will _never_ be refactored. It's simply not worth it. The vast majority of code will be, in a major way, the same until it's deleted or decommissioned.
